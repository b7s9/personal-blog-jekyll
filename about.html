This blog is dedicated to learning more about web design, development, usability testing, accessibility, and more.

My formal work experience includes working for the Philadelphia Water Dept. Public Affairs division as a web designer and developer, MGH Marketing in Baltimore as a web designer and developer, and Drexel University Student Technicians as a crew head, where I continue to work part-time during academic terms. In my spare time, I am an audio supervisor and sound designer for Drexel's Co-op Theater co., the RPM/Electronic director at WKDU Philadelphia 91.7 FM, and an experimental electronic/pop music producer.
